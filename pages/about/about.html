About Am-Guard Pro
Am-Guard Pro is ambulance contact app that is used to enable easy access to ambulances by means of cummulating contact numbers.

It enables us to contact the closest ambulances easily.

But the real success begins with you when you take action when a person is injured on the street and call an emergency service rather than just staring at that person.

Am-Guard Pro just an another way to help the society to help itself.

Special thanks to Shiva Shankar for the first iteration of Am-Guard without which this wouldn't be possible.
