How To Use
The app contains two ways to contact the nearest ambulance service.
  1. Using Pincode of location

  2. Using GPS and a map

Using Pincode

  • Type the pincode of the area onto the search

  • List of hosipital having the pin will emerge

  • You can call the hosipital you wish

Using GPS

  • Enable Geolocation on your device if not enabled and refresh map.

  • The map will zoom to the your location and nearest ambulance icons will be visible.

  • Click on the nearest ambulance icon from location to pop up call sheet.

  • Call the ambulance service.
